Classic Enduro Crashes

Chilly | Aug 03, 2013 | Comments 1

I don’t think I have run this one before. It popped up the other day in a search. I have watched it a couple of times before. I figured I would share it since it seems to remind me of my own racing lately. Keep an eye out for a couple of real heroes, Giovanni Sala shows up twice. There are some great classic bikes here too. I love all the minty KTM’s. I can’t imagine having to ride those old kickstart thumpers in some of these conditions. It must be about time for some of these gear fashions to come back in style again. – Chilly
